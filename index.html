Frequently Asked Questions

What is an AI detector and how does it work?

An AI detector is a tool that predicts whether a text was written by a human or artificial intelligence (ChatGPT, Gemini, etc.). Our system generates results by analyzing text predictability, sentence rhythm, and grammatical patterns.

Which AI models can it detect?

DetectAI; GPT-4, GPT-5, Claude, Gemini, DetectAI can successfully detect GPT-4, GPT-5, Claude, Gemini, DeepSeek, Llama, and over 50 other AI models. It can also detect outputs from paraphrasing tools like Quillbot.

How accurate are the scan results?

Our accuracy rate in tests is at 99.2%. Especially in academic and scientific texts, the false positive rate (mistaking human writing for AI) is below 1%. However, no detector is 100% accurate, so reports should be used as an indicator.

Are my uploaded texts or files stored?

Absolutely not. Your uploaded files and texts are permanently deleted from our servers immediately after the analysis is complete. Your texts are not used for AI training and are not shared with third parties.
